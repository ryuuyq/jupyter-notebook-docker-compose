
### 画图中文乱码解决

中文字体库拷贝至：/opt/conda/lib/python3.9/site-packages/matplotlib/mpl-data/fonts/ttf/SimHei.ttf

修改配置文件：/opt/conda/lib/python3.9/site-packages/matplotlib/matplotlibrc

```
font.family:SimHei,  sans-serif
```
